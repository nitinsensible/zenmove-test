About Us

“Zenmove is a peer to peer marketplace for moving and moving services.”
Zenmove is the coolest thing to come out of Los Angeles, Califoria since sliced bread, even though we're pretty sure that sliced bread didn't even originate here. Our goal is to make moving from place A to place B a whole lot easier. The reason we're trying to do that is because moving is really tough:

People Who Are Moving:

  • Heavy furniture is...heavy.
  • Do I want to rent a U-Haul for the tenth time this year?
  • Calling around to agents and property managers is a terrible hassle.
  • Coordinating people's schedules? Finding parking at viewings? Forget about it.

Service Providers:

  • Hey, I have a pickup truck, and big muscles.
  • My moving business, needs more business.
  • Make some extra cash setting up viewing appointments? Sure!
  • I know all the properties around town. I'd love to help you!

Zenmove brings these two sides together in one harmonious, awesome marketplace. Click here to see how it works!
